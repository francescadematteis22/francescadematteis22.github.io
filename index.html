Selected Projects

  • Family Climate and Teenagers’ Mental Health
    Empirical panel-data project based on the Understanding Society Youth Panel teaching dataset. Built and analysed panel data in Stata by appending waves, recoding variables, constructing outcome measures, and estimating OLS, fixed-effects, and random-effects models with Hausman tests.

  • Master’s Thesis (in progress)
    Construction of an original dataset from ANBSC administrative records on confiscated assets in Italy. Current empirical work includes survival analysis through Cox proportional hazards models and logit specifications.

  • Domestic Political Constraints and Environmental Treaty Effectiveness
    Research proposal developed at KU Leuven on how lobbying pressure and institutional capacity shape the implementation of international environmental agreements, with a theoretical framework and future empirical extension.

  • Bachelor’s Thesis on the Gender Pay Gap
    Literature-based thesis on the determinants of the gender pay gap, drawing on Claudia Goldin’s work and comparing evidence from the US and Italy.
